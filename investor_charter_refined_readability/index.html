Investor Charter in respect of Research Analysts (RA)

This charter aims to facilitate investor awareness and provide transparency regarding the services rendered by Research Analysts in the financial ecosystem, ensuring a fair and informed investment environment.

Our Vision

To provide high quality and unbiased research reports, empowering investors for informed decisions.

Our Mission

To serve as a trusted companion for investors throughout their journey in the securities market.

Details of Business Operations

01. SEBI Registration

Official registration with SEBI as a Research Analyst (RA). We strictly adhere to all statutory requirements and regulatory frameworks mandated by the Securities and Exchange Board of India.

02. Research Publication

Preparation and publication of institutional-grade research reports. Our analysis covers various financial instruments and is rooted in data-driven methodologies.

03. Security Recommendations

Providing evidence-based recommendations for securities listed on major stock exchanges. Every recommendation is backed by a comprehensive research rationale.

04. Advisory Services

Offering research-led advisory services to clients on a recurring basis, maintaining consistency in performance and communication standards.

Services Provided to Investors

  • task_alt

    Independent Research Reports

    Publishing comprehensive reports based on fundamental and technical analysis, ensuring complete independence from external influence.

  • task_alt

    Full Conflict Disclosures

    Maintaining absolute transparency regarding potential conflicts of interest, providing investors with all necessary context for our recommendations.

  • task_alt

    Verifiable Audit Trail

    Maintaining robust internal records and a verifiable trail for every recommendation released to the public or private clients.

Investor Responsibilities

The Do's

check_circle

Only deal with SEBI registered Research Analysts and verify their status on the SEBI portal.

check_circle

Pay fees only through official banking channels. Request receipts for all payments.

check_circle

Read all disclosure documents and research reports thoroughly before acting on recommendations.

The Don'ts

cancel

Do not entertain promises of "guaranteed returns" or "fixed monthly income" from any analyst.

cancel

Never share your trading passwords, OTPs, or demat account credentials with an analyst.

cancel

Avoid making investment decisions based solely on unofficial "tips" from social media channels.
