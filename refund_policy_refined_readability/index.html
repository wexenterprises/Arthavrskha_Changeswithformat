Refund Policy

At Arthavrksh, we are committed to providing institutional-grade financial insights. Please review our refund policy regarding our subscription-based research services.

01. Subscription Finality

Due to the nature of our digital research products and the immediate delivery of intellectual property, all subscription payments made to Arthavrksh are final and non-refundable.

Once access to our proprietary dashboard or research reports is granted, the service is considered fully rendered for that billing period. We do not offer trial periods for our premium research.

02. Cancellation Policy

Users may cancel their recurring subscription at any time through their account dashboard. Upon cancellation, the following terms apply:

  • check_circle Access to the premium research portal will continue until the end of the current billing cycle.
  • check_circle No further charges will be applied to the registered payment method after the effective cancellation date.
  • block Partial month usage or unused portions of a subscription term are not eligible for pro-rated refunds.

03. Erroneous Charges

If you believe there has been a duplicate transaction or a technical error resulting in an incorrect charge, please notify us immediately.

Contact our compliance department at compliance@arthavrksh.com within 48 hours of the transaction.

* Valid technical errors will be investigated and corrected via credit or refund at the sole discretion of Arthavrksh management.

04. SEBI Compliance

As a SEBI Registered Investment Advisor (INH000025212), our fee structures and refund mechanisms adhere strictly to the guidelines mandated by the Securities and Exchange Board of India.

We maintain absolute transparency in all financial dealings to ensure investor protection. Any conflict or grievance regarding payments is handled through our formal Grievance Redressal mechanism.
