01

Institutional Stewardship

Arthavrksh ("we," "our," or "the Firm") operates as a SEBI Registered Investment Adviser. Our architectural approach to privacy is founded on the principle that financial data is a sacred trust. This policy outlines how we curate, protect, and utilize information within our advisory ecosystem.

By engaging with our platform, you acknowledge the structural framework defined herein. We operate with a core philosophy: We do not sell your data; we architect its protection.

02

Information Infrastructure

To provide high-precision advisory services, we collect data across two primary vectors:

Direct Identifiers

  • • Full Legal Name & Identity Proofs (PAN/Aadhaar)
  • • Contact Information (Email, Phone, Physical Address)
  • • Financial Profiles & Portfolio Statements

Digital Footprints

  • • IP Address & Device Metadata
  • • Session Interactions & Learning Progress
  • • Transactional Logs & History
shield_lock

The "No-Trade" Guarantee

Arthavrksh enforces a strict institutional prohibition on the monetization of client data. We never sell, lease, or trade your personal or financial information to third-party marketing entities. Your data exists solely to facilitate professional advisory.

Architectural Integrity Verified
03

Systemic Utilization

A. Advisory Customization

We utilize your risk profile and financial objectives to architect bespoke investment strategies. This data is the raw material for our algorithmic and manual advisory tracks, ensuring recommendations align with your specific solvency and growth goals.

B. Regulatory Recordkeeping

As per SEBI mandates, we maintain rigorous documentation of all advisory interactions. This is a non-negotiable compliance requirement for institutional transparency and investor protection.

04

Security Architecture

End-to-End Encryption

We employ AES-256 bit encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Your financial records are secured within industry-standard digital vaults, inaccessible to unauthorized parties.

Zero-Trust Access Control

Only authorized advisory personnel with verified multi-factor authentication can access core client databases. We operate on the principle of least privilege access.

Independent Security Audits

Biannual security audits are conducted by reputable external cybersecurity firms to ensure our structural integrity remains uncompromised against evolving digital threats.

For inquiries regarding your data architecture or to request a record of information held, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@arthavrksh.com.
